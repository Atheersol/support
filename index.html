üîç Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can't log in to the app?

Make sure you're using the correct email address and password provided by your HR department. If you've forgotten your password, contact your organization's HR administrator for a reset.

Q: Location services not working?

Go to your device Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Location Services and ensure location access is enabled for ATS. The app requires location permissions to record attendance.

Q: How do I get the Odoo module?

Email us at support@atheersol.com with your organization details and Odoo version. We'll provide the module package and installation instructions.

Q: Can I use the app without the Odoo module?

No, the ATS mobile app requires our custom Odoo module to be installed on your organization's Odoo server. The module handles attendance data storage, processing, and management.

Q: Is my data secure?

Yes, all data is encrypted during transmission and stored securely on your organization's Odoo server. We follow industry-standard security practices. For more details, see our Privacy Policy.

Q: Who can access my attendance records?

Only authorized personnel in your organization (typically HR managers and administrators) can access your attendance records through the Odoo system.
